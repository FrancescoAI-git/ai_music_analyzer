# Mix — Drum & Bass

## Kick & Bass
– Kick leggero: corpo **55–75 Hz**, snap 2–4 kHz.  
– Sub sine **40–55 Hz**, sidechain minimo.  
– Reese bass: mids **150–350 Hz** da controllare.

## Drums
– Breakbeats high-pass **100–150 Hz**.  
– Transient shaper per definire l’attacco.  
– Clap/snare: corpo 180–200 Hz, crack 5–7 kHz.

## Mids & Pads
– Pads cinematici high-pass **200–300 Hz**.  
– Lead a volte aggressivi, controllare 3–6 kHz.

## Stereo
– Drums centrate.  
– FX e pad estremamente wide.

## Loudness
– LUFS target: **-7 / -6**.

## Compressione
– Parallel compression sul drum bus.  
– Multibanda per gestire reese senza schiacciarlo.
