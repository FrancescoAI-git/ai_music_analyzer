# Mix — Trance

## Kick & Bass
– Kick profondo **55–70 Hz**, click 2–4 kHz.  
– Bassline rolling, stabile, sidechain molto preciso.  
– Sub sempre mono.  
– Evitare accumulo 200–350 Hz.

## Mids & Lead
– Supersaw molto ampia: taglio **250–400 Hz** per evitare muddiness.  
– Boost presence **3–6 kHz** per il lead.  
– Pads high-pass **150–250 Hz**.

## High-end
– Top brillante, air **10–12 kHz**.  
– Evitare harshness sopra 8 kHz.

## Stereo
– Lead width sopra 250 Hz.  
– Bass e kick mono al 100%.

## Loudness
– LUFS target: **-7.5 / -6.5**.

## Compressione
– Multibanda leggero sui lead stack.  
– Glue bus per uniformare supersaw e arp.
